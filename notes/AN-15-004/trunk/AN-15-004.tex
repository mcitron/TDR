\abstract{This note summarizes the PHYS14 exercise for the inclusive 
search in final states with missing transverse energy and jets, 
using the $\alpha_{\rm T}$ variable. 
The studies of the trigger
strategy for the analysis during the LHC Run-2 are also presented. \\
Projections of the expected sensitivity for 
integrated luminosity of 3/10 \ifb at a center-of-mass energy of 13 TeV 
are shown for several simplified models of Supersymmetry.}
